About

Hi, I'm Matt, an aspiring Fullstack Developer. I graduated magna cum laude from the University of Colorado-Boulder in 2011 with a degree in Ecology and Evolutionary Biology with a teaching certificate in Secondary Science Education. I then worked as a wildland firefighter for the US Forest Service for nearly a decade, ultimately becoming a helicopter rappeler. This was a fun way to spend my twenties and taught me discipline, how to make decisions in high stress environments and manageing people. I am excited to start a new chapter in computer programming.
